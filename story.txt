This is the story of a person named Harold. He hase that overlooks a sizeable lake, but does not have a boat. He has been searching for the best boat that money can buy, but every time he attempts to buy this boat, a better boat comes along. He cannot have an oblolete boat, but he also can't be the only person that lives on the lake without a boat.
So he decides to build the best boat ever. Harold traves the world looking for the best boat makers to add to his team. After years of searching, he assembles the best boat making team known to man. After a couple more years of planning, the gang come up with the plans to make the biggest, baddest, most awesome boat ever. And then tragedy struck, leaving Harold penniless.  But that didn't stop the gang from making the bestest boat ever.They worked tirelessly until the boat came out perfect.
